Yuchen SHI 施宇晨 1

Greetings! I am currently a master student in Computer Engineering in Northwestern University.

I received my bachelor degree in Integrated Circuit Design in Huazhong University of Science and Technology. I wrote a small tool to compute optimal circuit parameters for analog amplifiers as my undergraduate project.

I enjoy coding and building. I also enjoy explaining what I know to people. I keep a blog written in Chinese. It mostly contains my personal thoughts about computers, physics and math. Someday I will write in English. I promise.

My resume/CV is here. You can open the link and print the web page in Firefox or Chrome to get a PDF version. 2

Experiences

I was a full stack intern, writing CSS and doing UI tuning in a local data visualization company in 2019 summer.

I am actively seeking a software engineering intern for 2020 summer. I have solved over 700 problems on Leetcode.

Projects

sizer

My undergraduate project. A small tool to compute optimal parameters for circuit components like analog amplifiers, logic gates.

Learn more here.

steve

A computer graphics course project. I made a naive 3D engine around WebGL’s drawing primitives to make my and my teammate’s life easier. This engine supports changing object’s material on the fly by on-demand, lazy loading of shader program. I also implemented a simple frame-rate-independent animation system to enable you to program key frames like CSS.

Learn more here.

simple-tcp

A computer networking project. I implemented a simple TCP-like protocol on top of UDP to enable reliable transport.

Learn more here.
